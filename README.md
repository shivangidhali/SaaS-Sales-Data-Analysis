# SaaS-Sales-Data-Analysis
## Project Overview
This Project ia a complete SaaS business analytics dashboard built using Power BI. The Dashboard analyze customer behavior, subscription revenue, churn trends, customer acquisition performance, and key SaaS KPIs to help businesses make data driven decisions.

## Business Problem
SaaS companies need to track:
- Revenue Growth
- Customer Churn
- Customer acquisition cost
- Retention performance
- Subscription plan performance

This dashboard provide insights into customer retention, revenue trends, and marketing effectivness.

## Tools & Technologies 
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Power Query
- Excel/CSV Dataset

## Key KPIs
- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- Customer Churn Rate
- Retention Rate
- Customer Acquisition Cost(CAC)
- Lifetime Value(LTV)
- Revenue Growth %
- Enterprise Revenue Share %

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Summry
- Revenue KPIs
- Customer metrics
- Business growth trends

### Revenue Analysis
- MRR & ARR Analysis
- Revenue by Country
- Revenue by Subscription plans

### Marketing and Acquisition 
- CAC Analysis
- Acquisition channel performance
- ROAS Analysis
- Trend analysis by Total customer & Total CAC
- Top Revenue Channel

### Churn & Retention Analysis
- Churn Trends
- Churn by plan type
- Retention Rate %
- Churn Rate %
- Churn Rate by Region

## Key Insights
- Pro drives 25% of total MRR
- Asia leads with 33%  of active customers
- MRR Growing 2.2% MRR
- UK Generates 8% of Total Revenue
- Refferal Has the highest CAC $161
- Refferal generates 17% of acquisition revenue
- Meta Ads customers show highest LTV at $286
- Enterprise plan show highest churn at 50.3%
- Organic customers show highest churn at 50.4%
- Oceania has highest reginol churn at 50.2%

## Recommendations
- Increase focus on Pro plan to maximize recurring revenue.
- Expand marketing efforts in Asia due to strong active customer base.
- Improve retention strategies to sustain MRR growth.
- Optimize UK market performance through targeted campaigns.
- Reduce high CAC in referral acquisition channels.
- Scale Meta Ads campaigns to attract high-LTV customers.
- Improve onboarding and support for Enterprise customers to reduce churn.
- Create retention campaigns for high-churn organic customers.
- Strengthen customer engagement in Oceania to lower regional churn.

## Project Outcome
This Dashboard helps SaaS business monitor performance, reduce churn, improve customer retention, and optimize marketing strategies.
